PC Hardware Interactive Simulator

Students identify parts, install components into the correct case and motherboard locations, connect power and data cables, then test whether the system boots.

Learning Objectives

  • Identify desktop hardware components and common internal connectors.
  • Install parts in the proper location and sequence.
  • Recognize motherboard slots and headers without placement labels.
  • Test a completed build by powering on the PC and monitor.
