PC Building Simulator Lab

Learn the major components inside a desktop computer, review what each part does, and complete a drag-and-drop PC assembly activity with automatic grading.

Learning Objectives

  • Identify major desktop computer hardware components.
  • Explain what each component does.
  • Place each component into the correct area of a PC build.
  • Use grading feedback to improve hardware knowledge.
